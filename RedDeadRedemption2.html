Red Dead Redemption 2 is a 2018 action-adventure game developed and published by Rockstar Games. The game is the third entry in the Red Dead series and is a prequel to the 2010 game Red Dead Redemption. You will explore the magnificent World and face many challenges in the cold snow and on the beautiful sunny days The main protagonist goes by the name of Arthur Morgan and he will make sure that you will fall in love with this game.
